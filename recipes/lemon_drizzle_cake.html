Lemon drizzle cake

Description

This wonderfully simple lemon drizzle cake recipe is super-quick to prepare. This recipe makes a lemon drizzle loaf, but if you want to make a lemon cake in a different shape.

    Ingredients:

  • 225 butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 225 g caster sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 225 g self-raising flour
  • 1 lemon, zest and juice
  • 85 g icing sugar

    Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C. Grease a 900g loaf tin with a little butter and line with baking paper.
  2. Whisk butter with caster sugar. Add the eggs one at a time. Add the flour and lemon zest and whisk until smooth.
  3. Pour the batter into the lined baking tin, flatten the top with the back of a spoon and bake for 50â€“55 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, stir together the lemon juice and icing sugar. When the cake has cooled a little, poke holes in the top using a skewer, chopstick or a piece of raw spaghetti. Drizzle over the icing, set aside for a few minutes, and serve.
