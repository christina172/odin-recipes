Lemon Surprise Pudding

Description

This lemon self-saucing pudding is a delight! Light, fluffy sponge with a zesty lemon sauce beneath.

    Ingredients:

  • 3 lemons
  • 100 g butter, softened, plus extra for greasing
  • 200 g sugar
  • 4 medium eggs
  • 75 g flour
  • 500 ml milk

    Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C. Butter a 2 litre shallow ovenproof dish.
  2. Grate the zest from two of the lemons, then squeeze the juice of all three.
  3. Cream the butter with the 100 g of sugar and lemon zest. Beat in the egg yolks one by one. Don't worry if the mixture curdles. Next beat in the flour a spoonful at a time, alternating with slurps of milk and lemon juice.
  4. Once that is all in, whisk the egg whites in a separate bowl, then gradually add 100 g of sugar and continue whisking until they form stiff peaks, and fold lightly into the lemon batter.
  5. Pour the mixture into the baking dish, then stand the dish in a roasting tin. Pour enough boiling water around the dish to come about halfway up the sides.
  6. Bake in the oven for 40â€“45 minutes until the pudding is golden brown. Serve hot or warm, with or without cream.
