Lemon butter biscuits

Description

These little lemon biscuits are so delicious and easy – you'll never need to buy biscuits again!

    Ingredients:

  • 125 g butter, at room temperature
  • 100 g caster sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 200 g flour, plus extra for dusting
  • 2 lemons, zest of
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 pinch sea salt

    Steps:

  1. Beat the butter and sugar in a bowl with an electric mixer until creamy. Beat in the egg until the mixture is light and fluffy. Add the flour, lemon zest, baking powder and salt and mix until you have a ball of dough. Cover and place in the fridge for 2 hours, or until firm.
  2. Preheat your oven to 180°C. Roll out the dough on a floured surface until ½cm thick. Cut out shapes and place on a greaseproofed tray. Bake for 15 to 20 minutes until the edges are light brown. Transfer to a wire rack to cool.
